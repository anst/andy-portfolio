About Me

Hey, I'm Andy Sturzu.

I'm currently a senior in high school and am extremely passionate about programming and great design. I love to pursue perfection to the best of my ability. I enjoy programming in Javascript and occasionally Python and Java . I stress (sometimes too much) over pixels. But what I love most about what I do is the most is the crossroad between development and design. I feel that it's nearly impossible to have a great product without both. That is why I try not to focus on one side of the spectrum, but both.

In my spare time I enjoy going to hackathons, participating in CTFs, playing tennis, listening to music, and watching TV.
